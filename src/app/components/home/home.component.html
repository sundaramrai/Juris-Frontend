I recently got into a minor car accident. What should I do?

For a minor car accident in India, you should:

  1. Stop your vehicle and ensure everyone's safety
  2. Exchange details with the other driver (license, registration, insurance)
  3. File an FIR at the nearest police station within 24 hours
  4. Inform your insurance company promptly
  5. Document the scene with photos if possible

Remember, leaving an accident scene is punishable under Section 134 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988.
